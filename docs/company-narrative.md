## 🧭 How to Talk About Us (style guide)

### DO say:
- "We build in public"
- "Practitioners who ship, not consultants who talk"
- "42 public repos, 28 live client sites"
- "Production code, not slide decks"
- "Open-source frameworks that we use ourselves"
- "2-person studio" or "founder-led studio"

### DON'T say:
- "Industry-leading" (we don't have the data)
- "Enterprise-grade" without context (we're 2 people)
- "AI experts" (overused, lost meaning)
- "Disrupting" / "Revolutionizing" (vague)
- "AI Whisperers is a..." → use **"We"** (we're a 2-person team, use first person)
