## 📊 Our Pricing Strategy Recommendations

Based on the 20-competitor benchmark, here's where we should be pricing:

| Service | Current Rate | Recommended Rate | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Web dev | $50/hr | $50/hr (keep) | Mid-market, competitive |
| Custom microservices | $80/hr | $80/hr (keep) | Mid-market, competitive |
| Consulting | $100/session | $150-200/hr | Below market, can raise |
| AI agent setup | Custom (TBD) | $5-15K + $500-2K/mo | Match market mid-tier |
| Generative AI integration | Custom (TBD) | $80-150/hr | Match market mid-tier |
| 3D render | Not priced | $500-10K per render | Below market, no competitor |
| Multi-tenant SaaS | Not priced | $10-50K + $50-500/mo/tenant | Premium, scarce capability |
| Workshop | Not priced | $500-2,500/student | Match market mid-tier |
| WhatsApp AI Agent | Not priced | $1-3K setup + $300-1,000/mo | New product, can set price |
| AI Readiness Audit | Free (30-min) | Free + $2-5K paid tier | Lead-gen engine |

## 🎯 Pricing Power Gaps

**4 places we have pricing power we're not using:**

1. **3D / satellite visualization** — no competitor, we set the price
2. **Multi-tenant SaaS** — high value, premium tier
3. **AI agent products (WhatsApp, etc.)** — subscription model, recurring revenue
4. **Workshops / training** — $5.5T skills gap = high demand

**4 places we're priced below market:**

1. **AI consulting** — $100/session vs $150-300/hr
2. **AI agent development** — custom pricing vs $80-200/hr
3. **Generative AI integration** — custom vs $80-200/hr
4. **Data engineering** — $80/hr (already competitive but could go $100-150)
