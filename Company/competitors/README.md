## 🏆 TIER BREAKDOWN

### Tier 1: Global Enterprise (5 companies)
**LeewayHertz, InData Labs, BairesDev, Itransition, Neoteric**

- Common: 6+ service categories, deep industry pages, "trusted by" logos, trust counters
- Pricing: Custom quotes (rarely published)
- Threat: LOW (different size, different market segment)
- Lesson: Service menu is wide (6-12 categories), deep (each has 3-5 sub-services)

### Tier 2: AI-Agent / LLM Specialists (5 companies)
**Master of Code, Intuz, Neurons Lab, Azilen, Maruti Techlabs**

- Common: "AI Agent Development" as flagship, framework-specific (LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen), 2024-2026 trend
- Pricing: $50-200/hr
- Threat: **HIGH** (most direct competitors on capability)
- Lesson: "Agentic AI" is the new category — we have a 2024 lead with `agentic-schemas`

### Tier 3: Custom Dev Shops with AI (3 companies)
**Appinventiv, Neontri, EffectiveSoft**

- Common: Broad service catalog (web + mobile + AI + cloud), vertical specialization
- Pricing: $50-150/hr
- Threat: LOW (they're not AI specialists)

### Tier 4: Boutiques (6 companies)
**Markovate, Voypost, Geniusee, Softura, Code & Pepper, Sloboda Studio**

- Common: Niche specialization, transparent pricing, 1-3 deep verticals
- Pricing: $25-150/hr
- Threat: MEDIUM (smaller, more agile, often better fit for SMB clients)

### Paraguay / LATAM (1 company)
**Baigency**

- Direct local competitor
- Threat: **HIGH** in Paraguay PYMEs
- Their weakness: no public GitHub, no flagship technical projects

## 🎯 Threat Assessment Summary

### HIGH threat (direct competitors, similar ICP, similar capabilities)
- **Master of Code** — portfolio, brand, similar AI agent focus
- **Intuz** — explicit "100+ AI agent deployments", similar positioning
- **Baigency** — local Paraguay competitor for PYMEs

### MEDIUM threat (different size/segment, but watch)
- **Neurons Lab** — production AI focus, premium tier
- **Azilen** — content engine + agentic AI
- **Markovate** — discovery-phase model, similar to our audit gap
- **LeewayHertz** — has ZBrain platform (productization play)

### LOW threat (different segment, different size)
- All Tier 1 (LeewayHertz, InData Labs, BairesDev, Itransition, Neoteric) — too big, wrong market
- All Tier 3 (Appinventiv, Neontri, EffectiveSoft) — broad shops, not AI specialists
- All Tier 4 (Voypost, Geniusee, Softura, Code & Pepper, Sloboda) — niche or budget
