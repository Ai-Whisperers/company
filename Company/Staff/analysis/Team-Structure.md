## Decision-Making Framework

### Decision Levels

**Level 1: Individual Decision**
- Within own domain
- Low risk (< $1,000 or minimal impact)
- Examples: Tool selection for personal workflow, documentation format

**Level 2: Pair Decision**
- Cross-domain impact
- Medium risk ($1,000-$10,000 or moderate impact)
- Examples: Client project approach, resource allocation

**Level 3: Team Decision**
- Strategic impact
- High risk (>$10,000 or significant impact)
- Examples: Major client engagements, hiring, strategic partnerships

### Escalation Path

```
Individual → Direct Pair → Full Team → External (Advisor/Board)
```
