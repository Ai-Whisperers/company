## Reference Etiquette

### Best Practices

✅ **DO:**
- Ask permission before listing someone as a reference
- Provide context about the job you're applying for
- Keep references updated on your job search progress
- Thank references after they provide feedback
- Offer to be a reference for them in return

❌ **DON'T:**
- List someone without their knowledge
- Provide outdated contact information
- Forget to follow up after the reference check
- Ask for references from current colleagues if job search is confidential
- Use family members or friends as professional references

## Reference Check Preparation

### What Employers Typically Ask

1. **Relationship**: How do you know Ivan? In what capacity?
2. **Duration**: How long have you worked together?
3. **Strengths**: What are Ivan's greatest strengths?
4. **Weaknesses**: What areas could Ivan improve?
5. **Performance**: How would you rate Ivan's performance?
6. **Technical Skills**: Can you speak to Ivan's technical abilities?
7. **Collaboration**: How does Ivan work with others?
8. **Reliability**: Is Ivan dependable and meets deadlines?
9. **Would You Rehire**: Would you work with Ivan again?
10. **Additional Comments**: Anything else to add?
