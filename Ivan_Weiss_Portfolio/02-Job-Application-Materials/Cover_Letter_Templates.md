## Cover Letter Tips

### Before Writing
1. **Research the Company**: Understand their products, mission, culture, and recent news
2. **Analyze the Job Description**: Identify 3-5 key requirements to address
3. **Find the Hiring Manager**: Address to a specific person when possible
4. **Review Your Materials**: Have your resume and portfolio handy

### While Writing
1. **Customize Each Letter**: Never send generic cover letters
2. **Lead with Impact**: Start with a compelling hook
3. **Show, Don't Tell**: Use specific examples and metrics
4. **Address Requirements**: Explicitly connect your experience to their needs
5. **Show Enthusiasm**: Genuine interest stands out

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Generic "To Whom It May Concern" greetings
- ❌ Repeating your resume verbatim
- ❌ Focusing on what you want rather than what you offer
- ❌ Spelling/grammar errors
- ❌ Exceeding one page
- ❌ Using the same letter for different companies

### Formatting Guidelines
- Use standard business letter format
- Keep to one page (400-500 words)
- Use professional font (Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman)
- 11-12pt font size
- 1-inch margins
- Save as PDF with professional filename: "Ivan_Weiss_Cover_Letter_[Company].pdf"

### Follow-Up
If no response after 1-2 weeks, send a brief, polite follow-up email reiterating your interest.
